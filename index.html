Berry Hunt !

How to play:

To play Berry Hunt you must move around the garden maze using your arrow keys, and collect the 10 strawberries! Shhh! One is hidden. Can you find the last one? (under health bar)

But Watch out! Dodge the bumble bees to keep your health up. One crawls towards you in anger, the other flies around minding its own business but if you touch it it will sting you! Also don't step into a rose bush or the thorns will scratch you!

The plants(maze walls) will push out to the other side if you get stuck in a bramble(if you try to move straight through them) this can be both helpful and bad as it can be a shortcut but also might push you into a rose bush or bee.

Don't worry, you can get your health back up by going into the white blossom flowers.
